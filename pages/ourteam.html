This website, along with all related materials such as the videos, were created by a team of five handsome young chaps from the UCL Computer Science department. Together, we worked to provide an informative set of articles along with a short video to introduce readers to the world of financial computing research.

Although we had no one leader, together we were able to manage time and work to meet internal deadlines promptly and get everything done to a tiptop standard.
